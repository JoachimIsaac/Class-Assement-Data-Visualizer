Class Assessment Data Visualizer

Visualize student assessment results over time and keep your department's data organized, comparable, and easy to explain.

Example trend preview (not real data)

How it works

1

Choose what to show

Select a Student Learning Objective (SLO), a Measure (what's assessed), and the academic years you want to compare.

2

See the trend

Press Plot to render the graph. You can show one or both targets, and tweak simple display options (like line/point size or colors).

3

Keep it current

Use Input / Edit Data to add new assessment results or update past entries so your plots stay accurate over time.

What you'll pick

Student Learning Objective (SLO)

What skill or outcome the course is aiming to develop.

Example: "S1 — Algorithmic Problem Solving."

Measure

How the outcome is tested or observed.

Example: "ETS Major Field Test."

Academic Years (Dates)

Full-year ranges shown on the plot.

Example: "2017–2018 to 2025–2026"

Loads in ≤3s. Plots in ≤4s. Designed for long-term retention and clear faculty review.
